A little about us

Everything started 6 years ago (2016), it starts the history and tradition of Shisha in Shkoder-Albania with 'Primo Shisha Lounge'. The thing who made us opening a shisha bar was that it would be something new to our citizens because there was not any shisha bar. So, we created an equip and we decided to bring something new. We decided to buy the latest type of shisha which is called high-tech shisha. By adding breakfast and different cocktails in the menu our bar become more attractive for our clients and today if you don’t call us and reserve your table before coming you cannot find a place. Thanks to our clients, which made us improve our skills and perfectionated year by year, and we will be here for you...Forever!

Discover our menu

Once you come to our shisha-bar you have hundreds of choices. The first menu contains all the list of our drink’s and our cocktail’s which are prepared by the best bartender in town. As you have chosen your drinks or cocktail a menu with more than 15 types of shishas will be proposed to you by the waiter. If you are hungry don’t worry, we have thought about it, you can choose 10-15 different types of sweet crepes. The best selling products are: Pineapple-Shisha, Blue Mist -Shisha, cocktails like Mojito, Mai Thai etc, a variety of different juices and at the and crepes with white or dark chocolate and those with fruits or pistachios.

Terrace vibes

During the summer our bar transformed into another form, what it means? Except for the indoor environment, during the summer the terrace will be open to everyone. With a stunning architecture, our terrace transforms every night into a pub, with a variety of different DJ’s no one would want to lose it. Our shisha and cocktails won’t miss there. So, if you are looking for a place to change your routine come to our lounge.
